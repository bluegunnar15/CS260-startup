Welcome to Dumb Questions!

The purpose of this website is to serve as a gathering place for those conterversial, but light hearted questions. An example of these types of questions include: Is water wet? Are there more doors or wheels on Earth? Fortunately for us the list continues as the users contribute!
Feel free to browse previously posted questions by clicking on the "Popular" tab in the menu bar above. For each post there is an opinion to vote and to comment. Please keep the comments respectful. We also encourage users to post their own questions/opinions! To do so, click on the "New Post" tab in the menu bar above.
When you are done, click on your name in the top right corner to sign out.

Thank you for visiting and enjoy!
